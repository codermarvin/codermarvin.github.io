About Our App

Fsr. is Field Service Record, a mobile app designed by Jehovah’s Witness for Jehovah’s Witnesses to record field service activities.

Simple

Simple, straight-forward and friendly interface.

Record

Records the basics—hours, placements, video showings, return visits and bible studies.

Tap and Swipe

Tap to edit and swipe left to delete.

Timer

Timer with hourly notification.

Calendar

Hours plotted in calendar.

Calls

Track your calls/visits.

Send

Send report via SMS, email or third party app.

Backup and Restore

Backup/restore via local/cloud storage.

Free of Charge

Free forever.

How The App Works?

Add

Tap the add icon.

Edit

Tap the item in the list.

Save

Tap the save button.

Delete

Swipe left on an item to delete.
