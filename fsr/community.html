Community Guidelines

App: Field Service Record  •  Provider: Marvin Aquino (Individual)

Effective Date: May 10, 2021

The Field Service Record community is intended to be a positive, encouraging, and respectful space. These guidelines help ensure a safe and uplifting environment for everyone.

By participating in the community, you agree to follow these guidelines in addition to our Terms of Service.

Our Community Values

  • Kindness and respect
  • Encouragement and positivity
  • Peaceful and considerate communication
  • Responsible and thoughtful sharing

What You’re Encouraged to Share

  • Uplifting experiences and positive moments
  • Encouraging thoughts and respectful comments
  • Content that supports a wholesome, supportive environment

What Is Not Allowed

Please do not post or share:

  • Offensive, abusive, or disrespectful content
  • Arguments, hostile debates, or provocative remarks
  • Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
  • Misleading, inappropriate, or harmful content
  • Spam, promotions, or unrelated material
  • Any content that violates laws or platform policies

Respectful Interaction

  • Treat others with dignity and consideration
  • Avoid judgmental, critical, or confrontational language
  • Do not impersonate others or misrepresent yourself

Moderation & Reporting

  • All posts and comments may be reviewed before or after posting
  • Content may be approved, hidden, rejected, or removed at any time
  • Users may report content they believe is inappropriate
  • Reported content may be hidden immediately
  • Violations may result in content removal or account restrictions

Your Responsibility

You are responsible for the content you post and how you interact with others. If you are unsure whether something is appropriate, it is best not to post it.

Updates to These Guidelines

These Community Guidelines may be updated from time to time. Continued participation in the community indicates acceptance of any updates.
