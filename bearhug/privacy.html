Summary (Plain English)

  • Bear Hug is designed to support wellbeing, but it is not a medical service and not for emergencies.
  • Your journaling and mood data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • Cloud backup/sync is optional, requires Apple/Google sign-in, and is available via a one-time in-app purchase.
  • We use Firebase for sign-in and optional cloud backup/sync, and Google Analytics (and related tools) to understand app usage and improve reliability.
  • We do not sell your personal data and we do not show ads.

Information We Collect

A) Information you provide

  • Mood check-ins (e.g., the emotion you select and any optional notes you add).
  • Journal entries (free-text content you type into the App).

B) Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information (such as device type, operating system version, language, and app version).
  • Analytics and diagnostics (such as screens viewed, feature usage, session duration, and crash/error reports), typically collected through Google Analytics for Firebase and/or Firebase Crashlytics.

C) Information for optional sign-in and cloud backup/sync

If you choose to enable cloud backup/sync, you may sign in using Apple or Google. In that case, we (and our service providers) may process identifiers needed to authenticate your account (for example, an account ID/token provided by Apple or Google).

D) What we do not collect

  • We do not display ads and we do not collect advertising identifiers for ad targeting.
  • We do not ask you to provide your real name, home address, or phone number for normal use.
  • We do not collect breathing-session content or “wellness exercise” content as user-entered text (the App’s focus is mood/journaling).

How We Use Information

  • To provide core functionality (store your mood check-ins and journal entries on your device).
  • To provide optional cloud backup/sync if you enable it (authenticate sign-in and sync your data via Firebase).
  • To improve the App (analyze aggregate usage patterns and fix bugs/crashes).
  • To communicate with you if you contact support.
  • To protect the App and users (fraud prevention, security, and abuse mitigation).

Storage & Sync

Local-first storage

By default, Bear Hug stores your mood check-ins and journal entries locally on your device. If you uninstall the App without enabling cloud backup/sync, your local data may be permanently deleted as part of the uninstall process.

Optional cloud backup/sync (paid)

Cloud backup/sync is optional and available through a one-time in-app purchase. If enabled, Bear Hug uses Firebase services to authenticate sign-in (Apple/Google) and store/sync your data.

Important: Cloud backup/sync depends on third-party infrastructure (Firebase and your Apple/Google account). While we aim for reliability, no service can guarantee uninterrupted availability.

  • Google Analytics for Firebase and/or Firebase Crashlytics (usage analytics and crash diagnostics).

Data Retention

  • Local data: stays on your device until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud data (if enabled): retained until you delete it via the App’s delete controls (or as otherwise required for security, backups, or legal compliance).
  • Analytics/diagnostics: retained for a period consistent with the analytics/diagnostics configuration and operational needs.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Use anonymously: You can use core features without creating an account.
  • Delete your data: You can delete journals and local data within the App.
  • Delete cloud data: If you enabled cloud backup/sync, you can delete cloud-synced data within the App.
  • Opt out of analytics where possible: Some analytics may be limited by your device settings; however, basic diagnostics may still be collected to keep the App secure and functional.
Note: If you uninstall the App without cloud backup/sync enabled, your local data may be permanently lost.
