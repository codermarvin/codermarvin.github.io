Frequently Asked Questions

What is Klu?

Klu is a word puzzle game where you must guess one hidden word using up to five clues. The challenge is to solve it as early as possible because fewer clues used means a higher score, and fast answers can also earn bonus points before the timer runs out.

How do I play Klu?

Each puzzle starts with the first clue and a set of letters in a letter bank. You tap the letters to fill the answer boxes and try to guess the correct word, with more clues unlocking one by one if you need extra help.

How is scoring calculated?

Your score is based on how quickly you solve the puzzle and how many hints you used. Solving the word with fewer hints gives more points, and beating the timer can award additional bonus points.

Can I still answer after all five hints are revealed?

Yes, you can still keep guessing even after all five hints are shown. However, solving earlier usually gives a better score than waiting for every clue.

What is the letter bank?

The letter bank is the group of letters shown for each puzzle that contains all the letters needed to spell the correct answer. It helps narrow down possibilities while still keeping the challenge fun and strategic.

Do I need an account to play?

No, Klu can support guest play so you can start immediately without signing in. Creating an account may unlock features like cloud save, leaderboard tracking, and progress syncing across devices.

Are there ads in Klu?

Klu may include ads depending on the final release version. These can help keep the game free to play, while optional purchases such as ad removal may be offered for players who want a cleaner experience.
