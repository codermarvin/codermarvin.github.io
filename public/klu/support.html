Frequently Asked Questions

What is KLU?

KLU is a word puzzle game where you must guess one hidden word using up to five clues. Fewer clues used means a higher score!

How do I play KLU?

Each puzzle starts with the first clue. Tap the letters in the bank to fill the answer boxes. More clues unlock if you need extra help.

How is scoring calculated?

Your score is based on speed and hint usage. Solving quickly with fewer hints earns the highest rewards.

Do I need an account?

No, you can play as a guest. However, creating an account helps you sync progress and track your place on the global leaderboard.
