Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play offline?

Yes! Previously played levels are available offline, so you can keep doodling even without a connection.

How do I earn gems?

Complete daily missions, beat your best times, or unlock achievements to earn gems for hints and power-ups.

What are missions?

Missions are special challenges like "Reach level 20" or "Connect 100 lines" that reward you with extra gems.

I'm stuck on a level!

Don't worry! You can use a hint to see the next move, restart the level for a fresh look, or try a different difficulty mode.

Will new levels be added?

Absolutely! We use a smart level generator to provide new challenges daily, ensuring you never run out of puzzles.
