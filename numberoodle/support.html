Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I play offline?
    Yes! Previously played levels are available offline.
  • How do I earn coins or gems?
    Complete missions, beat your best times, or watch optional ads.
  • What are missions?
    Missions are challenges like "Reach level 20" or "Beat your best time 3x" that reward you with gems.
  • I’m stuck on a level. What can I do?
    You can use a hint, restart the level, or try later with a fresh mind!
  • What do the stars mean?
    Stars reflect your performance on each level. More stars = better speed and efficiency.
  • How do I challenge a friend?
    Use the “Share Challenge” button to send a unique level link to your friend.
  • Will new puzzles be added?
    Yes! The game uses AI to generate new levels daily, especially in Hard mode.
  • Who can I contact for help?
    Use the contact form below or email us at numberoodlehelp@yahoo.com.
